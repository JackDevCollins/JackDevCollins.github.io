— PROJECT NAME 

Dm_Deeprock



— ROLE

Level Design & Environmental Art



— DATE

2019


DM_Deeprock is a deathmatch area for Unreal Tournament I made over the coarse of 5 months using the Unreal Engine. The map is a completely enclosed abandoned mining operation of luminous blue ore that help illuminate the map. This project was my biggest solo venture at that point and I learned a lot from the mistakes I made along the way. All assets came from Unreal Tournament’s default library.
